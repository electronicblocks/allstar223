    HOW TO ROOT J7 PRIME STOCK ROMS?
-----------------------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT REQUIREMENTS :-
 - A USB Cable
 - PC is needed

PROCEDURE :-
1. Get Latest TWRP Installed.
   - Click Here to download TWRP Recovery.
   - Click Here to Learn on installing TWRP.
2. Download Latest Magisk.
   - Click Here to download Magisk.
3. Connect your device to PC via USB Cable.
4. In your PC copy the Magisk zip to the InternalSD.
5. Disconnect USB Cable.
6. Flash the root zip.
7. Reboot your device & Enjoy.
