HOW TO INSTALL TWRP RECOVERY?
---------------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT REQUIREMENTS :-
  • USB Debugging & OEM Unlock Must Be Activated. It
    Can Be Found In Setting > Developper Option
    (Note:- Developper Option Can Be Activated By
    Setting > About Phone > Build Number. Click It Continuously to Activate)
  • USB Cable
  • A PC (A Computer) Needed
  • Download Samsung Odin (PC App) --> CLICK HERE
  • Download NoVerityScript.zip --> CLICK HERE
  • Download Latest TWRP Recovery
    • Click Here to download TWRP Recovery.
 PROCEDURE :-
  1. Power Off Your mobile
  2. Press and Hold Power, Volume Down & Home Button Together Until Download Mode Start Up
    (A Bright Blue Screen) Then Release all Key
  3. Run Odin on your Pc
  4. Connect Your Device Via Usb to Pc
  5. In AP Slot Put in The TwrpRecovery tar file you downloaded.
  6. Unselect All Options Which is Selected
  7. Flash It
  8. When Completed Disconnect USB Cable And Forget
    pc and PRESS & HOLD power+VolumeDown+Home Button Together Until Your Device Turns Off.
  9. As Soon as You Experience a Vibration
  10. Released ONLY The Volume Down & Quickly
    Press & Hold Volume Up While OTHER Buttons are
    STILL being pressed.
  11. When You see The samsung Logo Just Release all
    Button TWRP Recovery will Start Up.
  12. Press Wipe and Format Data and Type 'yes'
  13. When Wipe is Completed, Click on Reboot > Reboot RECOVERY
    (Warning :- DO NOT CLICK ON REBOOT SYSTEM
    ELSE REDO STEP 13)
    AND Just Stay In TWRP and Follow Next Step!
  14. Connect Mobile To PC Again
  15. Copy The NoVerity Script zip to your Mobile.
  16. Disconnect USB Cable & Flash The NoVerity Script Zip
    In TWRP
  17. Now See 1 Of The Following :-
    • - Root ONLY -> CLICK HERE
    • - Flash a custom rom -> WILL BE ADDED LATER

HOW TO UPDATE TWRP RECOVERY?
-----------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT REQUIREMENTS :-
  • Download Mixplorer Apk + Its Archive Plugin OR
    Use Any Other File Explorer Which Support .tar File
    (Es File Explorer For E.g)
 PROCEDURE :-
  1. Just Download TWRP Recovery tar in your mobile
    and extract it to your InternalSD with the downloaded
    file manager :-
    • Click Here to download TWRP Recovery.
  2. Reboot to Recovery.
  3. Click on Install & Choose FLASH IMAGE.
  4. Select the recovery img File you have extracted.
  5. Choose flash as Recovery/Image NOT Boot image,
  6. Finished. Now just Reboot Recovery.
